Improve Your Lifestyle

Victoria in action

BeFit will promote a healthier lifestyle for seniors in Victoria. We understand that seniors have a varitey of health concerns. The BeFit Victoria centre will provide a way for seniors to tackle these concerns and develop a healthy aging lifestyle. Our three main goals in promoting a healthier lifestyle are to help members stay healthy, receive rehabilitation and learn how to manage chronic diseases. This will be achieved within a supportive environment where seniors will feel welcomed and comfortable.

Remain Healthy Longer

Victoria is facing an increase in its senior population. This will cause a huge strain on our health care system. BeFit Victoria wants to tackle this by promoting within our centre ways for seniors to remain healthier for longer. We will provide a space where seniors will learn about healthy eating, experience catered fitness programs and receive medical assessments.

Rehabilitation

We want to help our members return to a healthy lifestyle after suffering various medical conditions, physical disabilites, mental health issues and more. We will provide a supportive envirnoment for members to work on their physical strength, cognition and mobility. Rehabilitation received at BeFit Victoria will help our members gain greater independence and confidence after illness, injury or surgery.

Chronic Disease Management

Chronic diseases can have immense effects on a person's wellbeing. This can impact someone's ability to be active and healthy. We hope to provide a proactive approach to chronic disease management through taking an active role in a member's care. Ultimately, we want to be able to give seniors the tools in which they can better manage their illness while maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

A Glass of Red Wine

Red wine has a long history as a form of medication. In fact, it is the world's oldest documented man-made medicine. In moderation, antioxidants in red wine may help prevent heart disease by releasing a certain cholesterol to help protect against artery damage.

Eat Healthy = BeFit

Eating a balanced diet of proteins, carbs and fats helps you keep mentally and physically healthy. Alos, fruits and vegetables provide essential nutrients that can help reduce the risk for stroke, cancer, heart diseases and type-2 diabetes.

Exercise to Your Fullest

Regardless of your age, sex or physical ability, exercise and health is yours to take and embrace. Individuals who adopt healthy active lifestyles significantly reduce the risk of being diagnosed with a chronic disease such as diabetes, coronary heart disease, stroke, and cancer by between 49% and 78%. Exercise can also improve your mood, boost your energy and help control your weight.
