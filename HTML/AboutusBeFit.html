Introducing BeFit Victoria

Working to Keep Victoria’s Seniors Healthy

Victoria is expected to see a 100,000 person increase in its senior population. As the number of seniors increases, the strain on our health system will increase too. We at Be-Fit Victoria want to tackle this problem head on and create a health centre which will help seniors keep fit and healthy.

The facility will promote a number of medical fitness programs designed to allow its members the opportunity to choose and promote a healthy aging lifestyle.

Why BeFit?

Something big is about to happen! The baby boomers are on the move. It's going to impact all our lives. It has never happened before and will never happen again. Change is coming and the impact depends on our response.

Senior Population Increase

Within the next 15 years, over 9 million baby boomers will officially become senior citizens in Canada. That’s up to 100,000 more seniors in the greater Victoria area alone. Seniors will represent 50% of the population in communities such as Oak Bay and Sydney. Alongside the demographic increase, seniors are also living longer than any other previous generation. This will make the 85+ age bracket the fastest growing segment of our population.

Social Care Increase

Changes in population demographics will see an increased demand for senior social care. These changes will have major repercussions for Canadians in all walks of life. Today, health care costs make up 50% of provincial budgets. Although seniors represent less than 20% of our population, they consume upwards to 40% of our health care costs. As the number of seniors exponentially increases, they will place unprecedented pressures on provincial health budgets. How will health care providers be able to cope with an increased senior population which is living longer?

Canadians Have a Choice

Will we just sit back and accept whatever our increasingly strained provincial health care system can provide? No, let's do something! BeFit wants to generate self-motivated active aging wellness programs that will encourage healthier life choices. Be part of a movement which is acknowledging this pressing issue in our society and providing a solution.

Health is a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being, and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity.

- World Health Organisation 1948-

In Manitoba, another company called Reh-Fit has created a centre designed to address the needs of an againg community in Winnipeg. It has an 80,000 square foot facility with 5,000 active members. Reh-Fit provides the best Canadian model for how we as a society can start to meet the demands of an increasing aging population

  • Enhance the health of members and community
  • Provide innovative health and medical fitness services
  • Provide medical assessment, education and exercise
  • Cultivate a supportive environment
