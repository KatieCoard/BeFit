BeFit Benefits

Victoria in action

BeFit will promote a healthier lifestyle for the aging population in Victoria. We understand that Victoria's aging population have a varitey of health concerns. The BeFit Victoria centre will provide a way for Victoria's aging population to tackle these concerns and develop a healthy aging lifestyle. Our three main goals in promoting a healthier lifestyle are to help the community stay healthy, receive rehabilitation and learn how to manage chronic diseases. This will be achieved within a supportive environment where Victoria's aging population will feel welcomed and comfortable.

Remain Healthy Longer

Baby boomers are about to favor Victoria with a significant increase in its senior population. An aging population can best help itself by developing a healthy and active aging lifestyle. Rehabilitation

BeFit will provide a professional environment to assist individuals of all ages to work on their physical strength, cognition and mobility, and return to a healthy lifestyle after an illness, injury or surgery. Rehabilitation received at BeFit will help the recipient gain greater independence and confidence following their medical intervention.

Chronic Disease Management

Chronic diseases can have immense effects on a person's wellbeing. This can impact someone's ability to nurture a healthy and active lifestyle. BeFit will provide a proactive educational approach to chronic disease management, giving people the tools in which they can better manage their illness and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

RECENT HEALTH NEWS

A Glass of Red Wine

Red wine has a long history as a form of medication. In fact, it is the world's oldest documented man-made medicine. In moderation, antioxidants in red wine may help prevent heart disease by releasing a certain cholesterol to help protect against artery damage.

Eat Healthy = BeFit

Eating a balanced diet of proteins, carbs and fats helps you keep mentally and physically healthy. Also, fruits and vegetables provide essential nutrients that can help reduce the risk for stroke, cancer, heart diseases and type-2 diabetes.

Exercise to Your Fullest

Regardless of your age, sex or physical ability, exercise and health is yours to take and embrace. Individuals who adopt healthy active lifestyles significantly reduce the risk of being diagnosed with a chronic disease such as diabetes, coronary heart disease, stroke, and cancer by between 49% and 78%. Exercise can also improve your mood, boost your energy and help control your weight.

About BeFit

BeFit Victoria will provide a facility to promote fitness programs aimed at keeping Victoria's aging population active and healthy.

We want the community to have the opportunity to choose and live a health aging lifestyle.
